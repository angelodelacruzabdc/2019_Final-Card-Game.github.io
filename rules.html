Rules

You must flip a total of 12 card pairs within a limit of 600 seconds. Be able to do that and you win the game. This is a game of concentration so you'll have to remember the cards carefully.

For Two-Player and Three-Player Games, you will be fighting with an opponent. Whenever an identical pair is flipped, a point is earned by the player which flipped the pair. A turn is lost when a pair of cards are flipped and they're not identical. However if you flip an identical pair within your turn you will gain a point and get the next turn too. The game starts with Player 1 then the turn switches to Player 2 when Player 1 loses a turn and so on. When Player 3 loses a turn, the turn will then come back to Player 1.
